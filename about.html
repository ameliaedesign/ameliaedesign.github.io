About me

Amelia Elizabeth Pike

Freelance Interior Designer & Student

Hello, I'm Amelia. I'm currently a student at Sheffield Hallam University where I study Interior Architecture and Design. My passion for Interior Design and creating a space where each client can experience the space differently inspires each one of my projects. I find great satisfaction in starting a project with a space with a specific purpose and seeing all the potential that the space gives. In my free time at University I had the privilege of creating a freelance business where I sketch and model bespoke furniture by hand or through Sketchup for designers and their live clients. I'm currently looking for more freelance opportunities and a 2022/23 work placement.
