About me

Amelia Elizabeth Pike

Freelance Interior Designer & Student

Hello, I'm Amelia. I'm currently a student at Sheffield Hallam University where I study Interior Architecture and Design. My passion for Interior Design and creating a space where each client can experience the space differently inspires each one of my projects. I find great satisfaction in starting a project with a space with a specific purpose and seeing all the potential that the space gives. In my free time at University I had the privilege of creating a freelance business where I sketch and model bespoke furniture by hand or through Sketchup for designers and their live clients.

Freelance Services

Furniture Design

I offer freelance bespoke furniture design which can be completed through hand-drawn sketches or using software such as SketchUp. Please get in touch for more details and pricing.

Interior Design

I offer freelance interior sketches or models through SketchUp or Vectorworks. If you're having a space refurbished and you need plans or 3D models please feel free to get in touch.
